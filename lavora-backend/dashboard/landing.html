FAQ

Common Questions

How long does setup take? ▾
Typically 24 hours from signing up to going live. We handle everything: provisioning your phone number, configuring the AI voice, connecting your Google Calendar, and training the system on your services.
Does it really speak Arabic naturally? ▾
Yes. We use the latest AI voice technology (ElevenLabs) with a custom Gulf Arabic voice — not a robotic tone. The assistant detects when a caller speaks Arabic and responds fully in Arabic for the entire conversation.
What happens if the AI can't handle a call? ▾
The AI is designed only for booking. If a caller asks a medical question, it says 'Our specialists would be best to advise you — shall I book a consultation?' It never guesses or makes up information.
Is patient data secure? ▾
All data is stored in your own Google Sheets and Google Calendar — you own it. We do not store patient data on our servers. Connections are encrypted via HTTPS. We comply with applicable Gulf data protection regulations.
Can I keep my existing phone number? ▾
Yes, in most cases. We can forward calls from your existing number to the AI system, or we can provide a new number. Both options are available at no extra charge.
